BeInTheRoom

Immersive AP World roleplay experiences where students enter historical decision points, defend perspectives, and turn choices into evidence-based reflection.

UNIT 1 · TOPIC 1.1

Advising the Song Court

Choose a historical role, enter a Song dynasty policy debate, and defend a recommendation using contextual evidence.
UNIT 2 · TOPIC 2.1

At the Caravanserai: Choices on the Silk Roads

Enter a 13th-century caravanserai outside Samarkand. Navigate profit, risk, and the politics of the Pax Mongolica as a merchant, escort, scholar, or tax collector.
UNIT 2 · TOPIC 2.2

After the Conquest: Life Under Mongol Rule

The conquest is over. Now decide how to survive — and possibly thrive — under Mongol administration as a scholar, merchant, monk, or local official.
UNIT 2 · TOPIC 2.3

At the Port: Choices on the Indian Ocean

Stand at the crossroads of Malacca, Kilwa, or Calicut. Navigate monsoons, diasporic communities, and the competing powers of the Indian Ocean world.
UNIT 2 · TOPIC 2.4

At the Court of Mali: Gold, Salt, and the Desert Road

Enter the court of Mansa Musa as he plans his legendary pilgrimage. Navigate the gold-salt trade, Islamic scholarship, and the politics of trans-Saharan wealth.
UNIT 2 · TOPIC 2.5

Ideas on the Move: A Scholar's Dilemma

Something has arrived along the trade routes — paper, a religion, a technique, a text. Decide how to respond to the ideas and technologies that connectivity brings.
UNIT 2 · TOPIC 2.6

The Plague Has Arrived

It is 1347. Ships from the Black Sea carry death along the same routes that once carried silk. Choose your role and decide what to do when the world is ending and no one knows why.
