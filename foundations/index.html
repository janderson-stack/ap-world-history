Pre-AP World Launch Unit

Before the Modern World

A short launch unit that gives students the essential pre-1200 context they need before entering AP World History: Modern. Each Foundations meeting has its own topic page with maps, lecture-card pop-ups, videos, modules, and checkpoints.

FOUNDATIONS 01

The First Civilizations

Why did civilizations emerge? Geography, agriculture, river valleys, specialization, hierarchy, and early states.

FOUNDATIONS 02

Belief Systems & Classical Thought

How did humans explain the world and organize society? Hinduism, Buddhism, Confucianism, Daoism, Judaism, and Greek philosophy.

FOUNDATIONS 03

Classical Empires

How did large empires rule enormous populations? Persia, Rome, Han China, and Maurya/Gupta India.

FOUNDATIONS 04

Trade Networks Connect the World

How did Afro-Eurasia become interconnected? Silk Roads, Indian Ocean trade, Trans-Saharan trade, and cultural diffusion.

FOUNDATIONS 05

The World by c.1200

What did the world look like right before AP World begins? Regional snapshots that bridge directly into Unit 1.
