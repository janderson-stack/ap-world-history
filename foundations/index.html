AP World Foundations Boot Camp

This launch unit uses the AP World themes as the organizing structure and pairs each day with an AP thinking skill. The goal is not to survey all ancient history. The goal is to give students the operating system they need before Unit 1: geography, belief systems, power, trade, social organization, innovation, evidence, and writing.

DAY 01

Geography Shapes Civilization

Humans and the Environment · contextualization and causation through rivers, deserts, seas, mountains, agriculture, and settlement.

DAY 02

Belief Systems & Cultural Exchange

Cultural Developments and Interactions · comparison through religions, philosophies, diffusion, adaptation, and shared values.

DAY 03

States, Power & Social Organization

Governance plus Social Interactions · causation through empires, bureaucracy, legitimacy, hierarchy, gender, and class.

DAY 04

Trade Networks & Innovation

Economic Systems plus Technology · systems thinking through Silk Roads, Indian Ocean trade, Trans-Saharan routes, diffusion, and innovation.

DAY 05

Thinking & Writing Like a Historian

AP Skills Boot Camp · claim, evidence, reasoning, SAQ practice, confidence reflection, and the bridge into Unit 1.
